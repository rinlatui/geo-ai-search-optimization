# 20. AI-Friendly Internal Linking

Internal links help connect entities, topics, and supporting context.

## Link Types

- Hub to cluster
- Cluster to hub
- Article to service page
- Author page to content
- Content to author page
- Organization page to services
- Related glossary links
- Supporting evidence links

## Anchor Rules

Good anchor text should be:

- Descriptive
- Specific
- Natural
- Connected to the target entity or topic

## Audit Questions

- Does the page link to its content hub?
- Does it link to related supporting topics?
- Does it link to author/entity pages where useful?
- Are links pointing to final canonical URLs?
