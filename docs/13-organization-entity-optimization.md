# 13. Organization Entity Optimization

Organization entity optimization connects content to the business or publisher behind it.

## Organization Signals

- Organization name
- Website URL
- Logo
- Contact page
- Service pages
- Social profiles
- Local business profile where appropriate
- Organization schema
- LocalBusiness schema where appropriate
- Author worksFor relationship

## Audit Questions

- Is the organization name consistent?
- Is the legal or brand name clear?
- Is the website URL canonical?
- Are social profiles connected?
- Does schema match visible content?
- Do author pages connect back to the organization?
