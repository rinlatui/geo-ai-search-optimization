# 18. Product / Service GEO Optimization

Product and service pages need clear commercial answers.

## Service Page Blocks

- Service definition
- Who needs it
- Scope of work
- Process
- Deliverables
- Pricing guidance
- Proof
- FAQ
- CTA

## Product Page Blocks

- Product definition
- Specifications
- Use cases
- Comparison
- Availability/pricing if relevant
- FAQ
- Trust signals

## Audit Questions

- Is the offer clear?
- Is the target user clear?
- Are deliverables clear?
- Are comparisons available?
- Is the CTA direct?
