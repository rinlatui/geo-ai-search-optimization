# 25. GEO Audit Checklist

A GEO audit reviews whether a page is technically eligible, understandable, extractable, trustworthy, and measurable.

## Audit Areas

- Technical eligibility
- Snippet eligibility
- Answer extraction
- Entity clarity
- Information Gain
- Fact density
- Source verification
- Author/organization trust
- Schema quality
- Internal linking
- Multi-engine visibility
- Reporting
