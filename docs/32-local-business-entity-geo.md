# 32. Local and Business Entity GEO

Local and business entity GEO connects services, locations, and organization identity.

## Local Signals

- Business name
- Service area
- Address if public
- Phone
- Contact page
- Map
- Google Business Profile
- Reviews where legitimate
- LocalBusiness schema where appropriate
- Local case studies

## Business Entity Questions

- What does the business do?
- Where does it serve?
- Who works for it?
- What services are offered?
- How can users verify it?
