# 22. AI Visibility Tracking

AI visibility tracking records whether a brand, entity, URL, or source appears in AI-assisted search experiences.

## Track

- Query
- Engine
- Date
- Answer summary
- Cited URL
- Mentioned brand/entity
- Missing or incorrect information
- Competitor sources
- Required content updates

## Important Note

AI visibility is volatile. Track patterns over time, not one isolated test.

## Output

```text
Query:
Engine:
Date:
Was cited? yes/no
Entity mentioned? yes/no
URL cited:
Issue:
Next action:
```
