# 17. Local GEO Optimization

Local GEO helps AI search systems understand business location, service area, and local relevance.

## Local Signals

- Business name
- Service area
- Address if public
- Phone number
- Contact page
- Map
- Google Business Profile
- LocalBusiness schema where appropriate
- Local case studies
- Local service pages
- Local FAQs

## Audit Questions

- Is the service area clear?
- Is the business entity consistent?
- Is contact information visible?
- Are local service pages unique?
- Are local proof signals present?
